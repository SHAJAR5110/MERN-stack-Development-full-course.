Traditional Recipes

Chicken Biryani

A fragrant and flavorful rice dish layered with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and basmati rice. This royal dish is perfect for special occasions and family gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 kg Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups Yogurt
  • 2 large Onions (sliced)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala powder
  • Saffron soaked in milk
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves

Instructions:

1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 2 hours.
2. Fry onions until golden brown and crispy.
3. Partially cook rice with whole spices.
4. Layer marinated chicken and rice alternately.
5. Sprinkle fried onions, mint, and saffron milk.
6. Cook on dum (slow cooking) for 45 minutes.

Rice Kheer

A creamy and delicious rice pudding made with milk, rice, and aromatic cardamom. This traditional dessert is garnished with nuts and is perfect for celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Basmati rice
  • 1 liter Full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 4-5 Green cardamom pods
  • Almonds and pistachios (chopped)
  • Raisins
  • 1 tsp Rose water
  • Silver leaf (optional)

Instructions:

1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
2. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan.
3. Add rice and cook on low heat for 45 minutes.
4. Stir frequently to prevent sticking.
5. Add sugar, cardamom, and nuts.
6. Cook until creamy consistency is achieved.
7. Garnish with nuts and serve warm or chilled.

Coconut Barfi

A sweet and rich confection made with fresh coconut, milk, and sugar. This traditional Pakistani sweet is perfect for festivals and special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Fresh coconut (grated)
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder
  • Chopped almonds and pistachios
  • Silver leaf for garnish
  • A pinch of food coloring (optional)

Instructions:

1. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan.

3. Add milk and cook until absorbed.
4. Add sugar and cardamom powder.
5. Cook until mixture thickens and leaves the pan.
6. Spread on greased plate and garnish with nuts.
7. Cool completely and cut into squares.
